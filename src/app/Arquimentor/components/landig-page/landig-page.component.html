About us

We are an emerging startup with significant growth prospects. In the initial phase, our user base may be limited, but our commitment and focus lie in leveraging the potential of technologies to expand our reach. Our current mission revolves around simplifying the process of aiding students who encounter academic difficulties, empowering them to elevate their performance and meet their educational goals. We will create a web application designed to assist students in locating teachers or mentors who specialize in the subjects they want to master and enhance about architecture. Correspondingly, our vision is to establish ourselves as a prominent solution for student support.
